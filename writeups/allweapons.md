## Category of the Week 252: :six: All Weapons :crossed_swords:
Want to spend an hour and a half to two hours playing hades the game using the strongest aspect of every weapon? That's what AllWeaps is.
Complete a run with one aspect of every weapon, preferably in a row. You will most likely playing the weapons in either courtyard-order, or in terms of consistency. Considering this, the most popular picks would be using demeter fists, then nemesis sword, then achilles spear, hera bow, beowulf shield, and either eris or hestia rail.

The [unmodded wr](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades/runs/y8j5k11z>) by tounis and the [modded wr](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades/runs/zp6o33gm>) by AllWebs... uh, I mean "webs2d"... are both available.
