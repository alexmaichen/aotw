## Category of the Week : :dash: Dash Only :dash:
Do you enjoy pressing only one button? This is the Category Extension for you! Pick up the aspect as detailed above, acquire Poseidon's dash and go to town! Build towards Sea Storm and anything zeus- or Poseidon-related you can find. Try not to accidentally press anything other than the one button you are allowed to press though, as that would not be in the spirit of the challenge!

As always, here is the [unmodded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades_ce/runs/yln4j1xz>) and the [modded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades_ce/runs/yd1k910y>), both by Guan Yeezy.
