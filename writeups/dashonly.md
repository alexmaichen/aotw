## Category of the Week : :dash: Dash Only :dash:
Do you enjoy pressing only one button? This is the Category Extension for you! Pick up the aspect as detailed above, acquire Poseidon's dash and go to town! Build towards Sea Storm and anything zeus- or Poseidon-related you can find. Try not to accidentally press anything other than the one button you are allowed to press though, as that would not be in the spirit of the challenge! What hammer one gets doesn't matter in the slightest,and the preferred Hermes-choices are the same as usual.

As always, here is the [unmodded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades_ce/runs/yln4j1xz>) and the [modded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades_ce/runs/yd1k910y>), both by Guan Yeezy.

Speaking of Poseidon dash on the heat side of Gilgamesh, the heat world record for Dash Only sits at 46 for [unmodded](<https://www.youtube.com/live/KeaNUP1vXRU&t=4483>) (by myself, AlexCA), and 41 for [modded](<https://www.twitch.tv/videos/2635460502>) (by Dust), so maybe someone could try to push those if speedruns don't sound like fun? Despite the restriction, Dash Only could go to above 50.
