# Aspect of the Week : :ZagPls: Zagreus Sword :person\_fencing:
The Blade of Zagreus, AKA aspect of Rush Delivery... more than other aspects, anyway. Its innate movement speed boost just pairs incredibly with *that* Hermes-boon!
You'll be looking to build either the powerful :MercifulEnd: like [Tounis'](https://www.speedrun.com/hades/runs/ylr3d62y) in his unmodded WR, or try the arguably more reliable (but trickier to build) Flat Damage Soup strategies like in [Zyru's](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UDSZZJMtXTw) modded WR, you choose!

Also consider equipping the plume at some point in the run for that sweet movement speed and more chances at Rush Delivery! :Dusa:
The only hammers an unmodded player might look for would be Double Edge and Piercing Wave, though running ME can make looking for the right hammers less essential.

For more build ideas, check out this [sword guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1GvdbSS3DrM0LHzZWWmWHt15Z87LFNUf-xUmH1_BMLps/edit?tab=t.0) by trash\_lapras.



## for heat
Merciful End is needy and is not something to go for the higher one climbs into heats. Go this route at your own risk.

The usual poseidon/athena dash -> FDS shenanigans tends to be a constant on the swords. Thus, [Retrash's unmodded WR (55)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fPQOZFgDlx4) goes this route.

Athena attack (+ poms) + World Splitter is an uncommon but underrated strategy, check out past zagsword writeups in this channel for more info about it! The basic gist is to get rid of the long special-animation, by having one's damage come solely from the attack (with the secondary benefit of getting rid of all but 1 runkiller-hammer, making 2nd hammer less terrifying to pick up), with some safety and damage provided by an athena or aphrodite attack. Do note that the attack-cycle on chopp becomes dashstrike -> attack, vastly different from the regular sword-rotation. This is the strategy used in [Scratch's modded WR (56)](<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_8DdryxNUbo>).
