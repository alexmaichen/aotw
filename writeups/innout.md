\## Category of the Week : :MelSalute: In-N-Out :ZagSalute:

\*Hades 2 gameplay-spoilers are marked and hidden accordingly.\*



Does AnyHeat feel dull on its own? Does it feel like a solved game? Here's something to spice things up: Playing both Hades games at the same time! Divide your focus among both games as necessary to win! There is one additional rule however to enforce the need to divide one's attention, and it is that progress must be made across both games as approximately the same rate: Boss-arenas must be entered in both games within a few seconds of each other.



Due to their similarity in animations, the meta for this category is to play Arthur in Hades 1 (preferably with the shadow slash hammer)) and ||Mel Axe (though this wants an attack-speed boost to make the animation speed similar to that of Arthur)|| or ||Thanatos Axe (which happens to have an innate 30% attack speed boost meaning it does not need rapid slash)|| in Hades 2.



This one's a collaboration with the Hades 2 side of the server! Normally, the Category of the Week for this aspect often remains unplayed, so let's do something unique instead. It is common for speedrunning communities involving multiple games of the same series to have a meme-category that consists of accomplishing an objective across multiple games. This CotW is just that! RTA timer starts when one of the games gain control and ends when the player has won in both games.



For Hades 2, CotW is beating the two games in succession instead of at the same time, which is less chaotic. If this is something you prefer, sum together the two IGTs of your runs and tag your run with #h2cotw14 !
