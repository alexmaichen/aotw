# Aspect of the Week : :zap: Eris Rail :gun:
Eris is one of the most facetanky aspects in Hades the game, and is therefore a common speedrun-beginner pick. Keep standing in your special for that innate rare Rush Delivery (kinda)!

Eris pretty much feels *designed* to run Flat Damage Soup!
More specifically, the preferred things to get are
Zeus: attack, Static Discharge + poms, Lightning Rod and Splitting Bolt
Artemis: special, Lightning Rod, and maybe some tier 2s
Poseidon: Dash + poms, Razor Shoals, Breaking Wave. Sea Storm is an option too but takes a while to build towards, making it unreliable.

Rush Delivery and dashes are both viable options.

Hammer-wise, one is looking for Rocket Bomb > Cluster Bomb > Triple Bomb > Delta Chamber > Targeting System > Flurry Fire (?) > everything else

[Wmic's unmodded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades/runs/yvo94xxz>) and [Webs' modded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades/runs/y60xoj6y>) both use Eris to its fullest effect using the aforementioned ZAP build.

Check out [Wmic's Eris guide](<https://docs.google.com/document/d/13lSPIUsiJGr5vApeon6ayX9N4-UlvGHWrCGdYAxhvHM/edit?tab=t.0>) for the in-depth mechanics and build-options.


# Aspect of the Week : :zap: Eris Rail :gun:
It's the week of Erismas, for those who celebrate! :mrs_claus: :Eris: Like any other Eris week, it's time to create the strife and madness real-life won't allow you to do!
This aspect is a common starting-point when learning to speedrun Hades (feel free to join us this week, even if you have never participated in AotW before :ZagGrin:), and for good reason: It provides Zagreus with effectively a rare Hermes Rush Delivery + Hyper sprint for free, along with a build that is easy to understand and get together.

Eris pretty much feels *designed* to run Flat Damage Soup!
More specifically, the preferred things to get are
Zeus: attack, Static Discharge + poms, Lightning Rod and Splitting Bolt
Artemis: special, Lightning Rod, and maybe some tier 2s
Poseidon: Dash + poms, Razor Shoals, Breaking Wave. Sea Storm is an option too but takes a while to build towards, making it unreliable.

Hyper Sprint -> Rush Delivery (Hyper Delivery) (for even more global scaling) and dashes are both viable options, though the Hyper Delivery boon-combination is significantly preferred.

Hammer-wise, one is looking for Rocket Bomb > Cluster Bomb > Triple Bomb > Delta Chamber* > Targeting System > Flurry Fire (?) > Piercing Fire > Ricochet Fire > everything else

\* Delta Chamber priority dependent on skill level; the better you are the less high it generally is ranked among the useful hammers. Still quite comfortable to play though.

[Wmic's unmodded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades/runs/yvo94xxz>) and [Webs' modded WR](<https://www.speedrun.com/hades/runs/y60xoj6y>) both use Eris to its fullest effect using the aforementioned ZAP build.

Check out [Wmic's Eris guide](<https://docs.google.com/document/d/13lSPIUsiJGr5vApeon6ayX9N4-UlvGHWrCGdYAxhvHM/edit?tab=t.0>) for the in-depth mechanics and build-options.
